Castle Defense has been released new version v2.4.42  by 6 May 2022

Update:
1. Critical bug fix: in some certain conditions, game use the wrong attack data for all attacks.
2. Upgrade new image for Dark Galio.
3. Add Simulation feature in attack editor.
4. Add new challenge rule:
-Player can play against one opponent one time per day.
-He can play against the same opponent one more time on same day, but will get penalty as cannot play against that opponent on next day.
-If user do so, in next 2 days, player can attack that opponent again (back to no.1).
