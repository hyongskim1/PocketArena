Castle Defense has been released new version v2.4.8  by 13 Apr 2022

Update:
Change the XP sharing structure when completing an Arena battle.

*Current structure:
- When an Arena battle is completed → Winner take both XP entry fees (100% of both XP entry fee).
* New structure:
When an Arena battle is completed → 
- Winner take 80% of both XP entry fees.
- Loser take 20% of both XP entry fees.
